Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Meditation Bell Timer mobile application (herein referred to as the "App") for Android.

We believe that your meditation practice is entirely your own business. Therefore, we have built this App with a strict privacy-first, offline-first approach.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No account creation is required to use the App.
  • We do not track your usage, session lengths, or audio preferences.
  • We do not collect crash logs or device identifiers.

2. Offline Functionality

The App is designed to work 100% offline. It does not connect to the internet to function, and it does not communicate with any external servers or databases. All of your settings are saved locally on your own device.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Tracking

Meditation Bell Timer is a premium, paid application. Because you pay for the App upfront, there are absolutely no advertisements and no third-party tracking SDKs (such as Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel) embedded in the code.

4. App Permissions

The App only utilizes standard, non-sensitive device features necessary to run the timer and play background audio. The App does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or personal files.

5. Children's Privacy

Because the App does not collect any personal information from anyone, it inherently protects the privacy of children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. However, because our core philosophy is to never collect user data, any future updates will only be to reflect changes in legal requirements or new App features, not to introduce data collection. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
